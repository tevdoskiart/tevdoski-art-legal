Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Tevdoski Art ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Basic TikTok account information (username, profile picture)
  • Videos you choose to publish through our app
  • Usage data to improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • To operate and improve the Tevdoski Art application
  • To communicate important updates

4. TikTok Integration

Our app integrates with TikTok via the official TikTok Content Posting API. When you connect your TikTok account:

  • We only post content that you explicitly authorize
  • We do not store your TikTok password
  • You can revoke access at any time through TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions
  • We comply with TikTok Platform Terms and Developer Policies

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our core service.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for TikTok integration at any time
